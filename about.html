Dr. Melissa Garcia, PT, DPT, CSCS

I’m a Doctor Physical Therapy and Strength Coach with a passion for holistic healthcare and leveraging technology to improve patient outcomes. My journey began in orthopedics, where I gained valuable experience treating clients with a wide range of acute and chronic conditions. Concurrently, I had the privilege of serving veterans in critical care and inpatient settings at the VA, deepening my understanding of the complexities of healthcare delivery.

Witnessing the challenges my clients faced in managing their health and accessing proper care, I transitioned into health tech, focusing on Customer Success. In this role, I've had the opportunity to make a larger impact on individual patient outcomes through innovative health technologies.

Through my experiences in the healthcare technology industry, I've cultivated invaluable connections and continue to learn and grow, driven by a commitment to improving healthcare accessibility and quality on a larger scale.

I’m excited to leverage my clinical skills not only to serve clients in my private practice but also to provide invaluable insights guiding strategic business decisions, product development, and content production.
